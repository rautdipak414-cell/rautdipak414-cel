About Shahi Bhoj

Our Story

A Royal Dining Tradition

Shahi Bhoj started with a simple dream — to bring the authentic flavors of Rajasthan to the heart of Aurangabad. What began as a humble thali restaurant has grown into one of the most celebrated dining destinations in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar.

Our thali is a royal feast — combining the best of Rajasthani (Dal Baati Churma, Gatte, Ker Sangri), Gujarati (Kadhi, Dhokla, Farsan), and Maharashtrian (Puran Poli, Amti, Bhakri) cuisines in one unlimited spread.

With over 6,000 five-star reviews and a loyal fanbase, Shahi Bhoj continues to serve with love, warmth, and the promise of an unforgettable meal.
